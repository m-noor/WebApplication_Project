Welcome to Outdoor Adventures Store

A premier store catering for sports-related equipment and clothing

Our History

For more than three decades, Outdoor Adventures have been providing thrill-seekers with quality items at competitive prices. From a humble beginning on O'Connell Street, Dublin begun by Tom and John McKinsley, we are now the leading clothing and equipment store in Ireland.

Our Collection

The secret to our extremely low prices? We obtain our merchandise directly from the manufacturers, cutting down on the middle-man costs. We carry brands such as Helly Hansen, Craghoppers and Karrimor. Can't find what you need? Contact us for a custom order where we will source it for you from less-mainstream brands.

Our Guarantee

We guarantee every piece of clothing and equipment you buy from us, whether online or in store, comes with a 12-month warranty. It's broken? Come talk to us. Not happy? Get full refund, no question asked.
